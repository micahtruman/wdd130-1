Overview

Purpose

I would like to make a website or blog that sells digital art prints and NFTs. I think everyone should have art in their homes and my website will help provide people with many options of art that they can order. I want it to be for independent artists that want to have a platform to sell their art and make the most money. I will charge a $4 service fee for every transaction that’s it though, all the rest of the money will go to the artist. I think it will be a site like ETSY or eBay. I want it to have that layout. It can help me earn some money and help artists make money and share their art. Feedback from other: I talked to my dad about it, and he liked the idea, he said though how will I be able to sell stuff when there is like Amazon that can sell art prints and stuff too, so why would people come to my site? I said because I guess my site will be more niche and not as big as Amazon, I will only have art for sale on the website. I talked to my mom about it and she liked it and didn’t really have much bad to say about it. She has a successful blog, and she gave me some tips and stuff and she said it was good to have it narrowed down to a specific niche, it would help me find more specific customers. I asked my friends about it and they thought it was a good idea and that since like NFT’s and stuff is kind of big right now that people would like that, and they think art is cool to and that more people should own art work, they like illustration though not really like oil painting like me.

Audience

My intended audience would probably be younger people, most likely in the age range of 14-44 and mostly males. I think it could be for art students or anyone that likes to just look at art!
